# Build journal

A running log of building this app, one entry per milestone, written the same sitting the milestone ships (TASKS.md rule 6). Newest entry on top.

Two audiences read this later via the portfolio: **web developers** (who want the technical story — decisions, trade-offs, things that bit me) and **hiring managers** (who want the human story — a real problem, scoped and shipped, lessons drawn). Write for both by being concrete and honest; polish is the enemy here, hindsight-smoothing doubly so.

Entry template — copy, fill, delete what doesn't apply:

```markdown
## YYYY-MM-DD — M_: <milestone name>

**Shipped:** what exists now that didn't before (one or two sentences, link the commit/tag).
**Learned:** the concept or skill that was new — explained the way I'd explain it to past-me.
**Tripped on:** what went wrong or surprised me, and how I got unstuck.
**Decided:** any choice I made and why (or "followed the spec, no forks in the road").
**Time:** actual vs. the TASKS.md estimate.
```
